I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), Hefei, China. Previously, I obtained my Ph.D degree from School of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) in 2023, supervised by Prof. Meng Wang and Prof. Dan Guo. Prior to that, I received my B.E. degree from Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) in 2017. My research interests include image/video understanding and its interaction with natural languages, with a special focus on Image/Video Captioning.
